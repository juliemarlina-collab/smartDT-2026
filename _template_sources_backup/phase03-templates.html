T07 Brainstorming Sheet
Generate at least 20 ideas before judging or selecting.
Not Started
A1
Interactive Activity
Rule: no judging during brainstorming.
Goal: quantity first, quality later.
Team task: vote and choose top 5 ideas after 20 ideas are created.
Sample Brainstorming Prompt

HMW: How might we help students get lunch faster during peak hours?

  • Pre-order lunch form
  • Queue number screen
  • Lunch pickup shelf
  • Student lunch buddy system
  • Popular meal prediction board
Brainstorming Rule

Do not delete wild ideas too early. Sometimes weak ideas become useful after combining with other ideas.

T08 SCAMPER Sheet
Use 7 SCAMPER lenses to stretch and improve ideas.
Not Started
A2
Interactive Activity
Choose one idea: use a top idea from T07.
Apply lenses: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to use, Eliminate, Reverse.
Select: identify the best improved idea after SCAMPER.
Sample SCAMPER

Idea: Pre-order lunch form

Combine: Combine pre-order with pickup time slots.

Eliminate: Remove repeated manual order writing.

Reverse: Instead of students queueing, meals are prepared by pickup batch.

T09 Idea Selection Matrix
Score top 5 ideas using feasibility, impact, novelty and cost.
Not Started
A3
Interactive Activity
Score objectively: use 1 to 5 for each criterion.
Compare: totals help the team discuss, not blindly decide.
Sample Matrix Criteria

Feasibility: Can we build this with our time/resources?

Impact: Does it solve the user need strongly?

Novelty: Is it creative or different enough?

Cost: Is it affordable? Higher score = lower/easier cost.
