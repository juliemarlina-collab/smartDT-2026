Sample Prototype Plan

Prototype name: Smart Lunch Pre-Order Mockup

Type: Low-fidelity digital form + pickup flow sketch

What it demonstrates: How students order before break and collect food faster.

Success criteria: Users understand the ordering flow without long explanation.

Prototype Mindset

A prototype is a question, not a final answer. Build fast, test early and improve from feedback.
