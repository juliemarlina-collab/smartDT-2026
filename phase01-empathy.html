Empathy Phase

T01 → T02 → T03 → T04
Visual GuideINFOGRAPHIC OVERVIEW
1DEFINITION

The Empathy Phase helps students understand real users, their experiences, feelings, challenges and needs through observation and interaction.

2PURPOSE
👥Understand the user context
🎯Identify real pain points
💬Listen to user stories and experiences
💡Build meaningful insights before defining the problem
3STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS
1T01

Observe with POEMS

Look at People, Objects, Environments, Messages and Services.

2T02

Interview the User

Ask open-ended questions and record important responses.

3T03

Transcribe the Findings

Turn audio or notes into written evidence and quotes.

4T04

Build an Empathy Map

Organise findings into Say, Do, Think and Feel.

🏆
Key Output

Students understand who the user is, what the user experiences and what matters most to the user.
